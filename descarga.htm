Game Information

Get the beats on in this fan-based rhythm game by nullrefrepro. Play the cute and sweet Gura/Kiara Hololive rhythm game in Hinotori Rhythm! Bright and colorful, the rhythm would not leave you. Prepare for rock and rolling good time So, step into your school girl shoes, bring your dolphin with you. Jump and run to collect some notes!

Get Ready for a rocking good time. Hinotori Rhythm is a side-scrolling game where you play as a young girl trying to collect musical notes. With Japanese pop songs in the background, you will be hard-pressed not to nod your head in this musical game. Accompanied by your trusty sidekick, a penguin in a bubble. Run through the halls and collect musical notes to string together combos and beat your opponent. Run through the game with lightning speeds. Your enemies will try to stop your progress by shooting globs of water at you, so be on your guard. Reminiscent of old dance revolution games, you must keep the beat going as the screen gets hectic. The score is shown on the left-hand side of the screen to tell you if you have the highest number of combos strung together at the end of the level to test your hand and eye coordination skills. Once the origamis appear, collect them all to unleash a devastating musical attack! Hinotori Rhythm combines the music of most arcade games that you can readily play on your browsers.
